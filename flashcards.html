Flashcards

I like flashcards. A lot. I generate them using Flashup, a simple markup language with a PDF translator.

This page is updated regularly, including revisions to files already posted. If you want to keep abreast of changes you should watch my repository on GitHub.

I print directly on index cards. I will be adding an 8.5" x 11" output mode for printing on standard paper or the expensive perforated kind.

I welcome feedback on the visual appearance of the PDFs, or on the flashup markup language. I also appreciate notifications of typos or mistakes in the cards themselves.
